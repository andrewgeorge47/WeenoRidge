Tips & Definitions

Playing Season

The main golf season when weather is favorable and you're actively playing courses. Focus on course time with strategic practice.

Grind Mode

Focus on technical improvement and skill development during off-season or when working on specific improvements to your game.

What counts as Short Game?

Chips, pitches, bunker shots, and any shots within 50 yards of the green.

Physical/Mental Prep

Includes fitness, mobility work, visualization, course strategy planning, and mental routines.
